# Security Model

UCII is designed around cryptographic trust rather than credential databases.

---

## Security Principles

### Identity Separation

Identity exists independently from authentication.

---

### Cryptographic Credentials

Credentials are cryptographic objects and can be rotated or revoked.

---

### Post-Quantum Protection

UCII incorporates post-quantum cryptographic algorithms.

---

### Economic Authorization

x402 protects infrastructure operations by requiring payment authorization.

---

### Replay Protection

Settlement receipts are tracked to prevent payment replay.
